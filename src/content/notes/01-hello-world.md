This space serves as my digital lab notebook. 

While my public broadcasts and essays on data science and grassroots politics live over at my Substack, this specific corner of the internet is built for raw exploration. 

My background is rooted in the humanities, political campaigning, and the Civil Service. Navigating the compliance structures of the Ministry of Defence and analysing the mechanics of elections taught me how complex human systems operate. However, observing these systems is no longer enough; I want to build them.

As I progress through my BSc in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, this log will document the technical side of that pivot. Expect code snippets, database design philosophies, explorations of agentic workflows, and thoughts on how we govern the autonomous systems of the near future.

The pursuit of purpose continues.
